    Purple Music Electronic music genre, evolved from dubstep, tells a story in approximately 2 minutes or less. Every 8 bars a new layer needs to be introduced with the intention of filling all frequencies with music layers until the final 8 bar chorus completely saturates the listener with sound. Track usually starts with the intended chord progression, adds beat, then bass line and so on until crescendo. Typically Purple tracks are written and played at 140 beats per minute. Purple Music should be able to have the beat removed and still remain interesting.
