phonology rough draft, essay format? Musical phonology is a phrase even google has trouble bringing in results for. One might find the recorded works of Leonard Bernstein among the few relevant results. One can occasionally hear this phenomenon in modern music, most recently I stumbled across Saul Williams, List of demands and reparations. His intent is made easier to connect to musical phonology when the melody changes from the hard synth to the singers voices mimicking the common bit of music taken from our language.
